# Court Bookings and Pricing

The Padel Company is an information platform, directory, and community hub mapping India's padel courts, coaches, and communities. It does **not** own or operate any padel courts directly, nor does it handle direct court bookings.

## Court Booking Prices and How to Book
* **Pricing**: Court booking prices are set by individual venue operators and typically range from **₹800 to ₹2,500 per hour** across India, depending on the city, facility quality, and time of play (prime time vs. non-prime time).
* **How to Book**: To book a padel court, you should contact the specific venue directly or use sports booking platforms popular in India, such as **Hudle**, **Playo**, or **KheloMore**. You can browse live courts in your city on the platform to find a convenient location.
