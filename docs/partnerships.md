# Partnerships, Listings, and Franchises

The Padel Company is an Indian padel ecosystem directory, media platform, and community organizer. It does **not** operate a traditional physical court franchise model of its own.

## How to Partner with The Padel Company
If you are looking to start, grow, or feature a padel operation in India, you can establish a partnership in several ways:

1. **Court & Club Operators**: If you run a padel venue, you can get it mapped and listed on the platform city guides for free.
2. **Coaches & Academies**: Get featured in the national coach directory to reach local players looking to book sessions.
3. **Tournament & Event Organizers**: Partner to promote local events, WhatsApp player communities, and citywide tournaments (like the Pune Padel Open).
4. **Brands & Sponsors**: Collaborate on brand placements, equipment listings, and event sponsorships.

For all partnership, court building support, or listing inquiries, contact the team directly at **hello@thepadelcompany.in** or use the contact form on [thepadelcompany.in](https://thepadelcompany.in).
